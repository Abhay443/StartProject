Communication Mediums

In the State of disaster, we can't bet things on the network as they are the first thing to get affected. So overcome the shortcome, our medium of communication would be GPS.

Today, every phone in the Era of SmartPhones are equipped with the GPS technology. We can harness this piece of tech in far better manner.

It can use the geoposition of the user generated by the GPS as a plot point which could be used by the rescue team to locate and save lives.

Ambiguity & Probabilities

The ambiguity felt was the phone batteries in the crisis situations. Its obivous that the power for the place would be off and battery might drain out.

But what if, out of 100 atleast 5 phones has battery and this could work as a boon for every one struck at that location.

The Odds are 5 out of 100, but yes even 5 SOS from that location could warn the officials and rescue team which could save plenty of lives.

We have even provided a emergency mode which terminates the apps in the background which would increase the battery stand point which would further help in appropriate location and mitigations of the people.

Even the odds of age group using smartphone is low, but a single warning from any age group would be highly important and works in our way.
