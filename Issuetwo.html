Mission Mitigation App

It would be a two-user type application which could be harnessing the XYZ maps for helping with the consequences of the disaster that has hit the place. It would be packed with some amazing mitigation options that would help the users to coup up the aftermath of the disaster that has shaken the place.

"SOS using GPS" - A message would be transmitted using GPS [Global positioning system] to the responsible authorities nearby like Government rescue team, Volunteers as well as NGO’s nearby.
"Help Nearby" - The pointer would show the nearby help available using the GPS and the Realtime data generated by the nearby help devices.
"Safehouse Nearby" - The pointer would show the nearby safehouses available which user could use as a temporary base in mitigation plan. This house would be marked as per the partner data as well as Realtime data [Facebook- crisis feature data, google safe feature data, safe marks by Here mitigation App etc.]
"Here Special Exit Routes" - Here will push out special exit routes for the affected area to be used for exiting the affected location. This would be made of XYZ Realtime visualized data.
"Food House & Medical Camps Nearby" - The pointer would show the nearby food houses as well as the medical centers.

"Find my Mate" - In state of Crisis, the most important thing is the connection with our acquaintances. "Find My Mate" feature records the safe marked users as well as the people that reach safe houses. It would even hold the records of the deceased person from the temporary established morgue which would at least help in overcome the trouble of searching them in such adverse situation.
"Mark me Safe" - Mark me safe is the feature that indicates that the user is now safe and allows terminating its help request send via SOS.
"SOS using GPS" - Even a responsible person might get in trouble in such situation, SOS is a message that be transmitted to another responsible rescue team nearby to help him out.
“The Nearby SOS picked by GPS” - It would be used to locate and recue the folks in trouble nearby.
“Mark this Place” - It marks as place as Safehouse for the users nearby by an authorised official for temporary situation.
“Mark this Way Secure” - It marks the route safe which could be used in evacuation of affected users nearby by all the volunteers & NGO’s nearby.
"Mark this Place as Food House & Medical Camp" - Marks the Inevitable food & medical points nearby which are the most important resource during a disaster.
“Death Records” - Its a feature where a registered Volunteer and rescue team can make entry for dead counts with the identify [if confirmed]
